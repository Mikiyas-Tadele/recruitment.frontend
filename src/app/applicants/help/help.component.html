Please follow the following steps to apply for a vacancy at Development Bank of Ethiopia

  1. Please check for a vacany in which you are eligible
  2. Register to create account to apply for a vacancy. Note: Please provide valid email address
  3. Email verification link will be sent to your email. Please open the link to activate and verify your account
  4. After you activate your account please login to build your profile and apply for a vacancy.
    NB: you will only be required to build your profile once and you can apply to multiple vacancies using your profile
  5. Once you build your profile visit the vacancies dashboard to apply for the vacancy you are eligible.
  6. Please write an application letter on the text editor and click aply to submit your application
